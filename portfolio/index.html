About Studio Varaždin

Studio Varaždin — operating under Lotus RC, led by director Timon Terzić — is a cinematic production studio based in Varaždin, Croatia. The studio specialises in documentary film, immersive physical experience design, music video production, and AI-augmented storytelling.

"Story first, technology second. Every project begins with a human emotion, a dramatic tension, or a journey."
